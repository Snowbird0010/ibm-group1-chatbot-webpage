Credit for Prior Learning

The Credit for Prior Learning (CPL) program helps students turn real-world experience into academic credit, saving time and money on their degree. Our chatbot guides you through eligibility, documentation, and next steps to make the CPL process simple and stress-free.

CPL Pathways
  • 1 Course Waiver for PJM5900 - Foundations of Project Management
  • 2 Transfer Credit for Prior Coursework
Important This chatbot provides an initial estimate of your CPL eligibility. An advisor will provide the final decision once you submit your full application.
Tip for using the chatbot:
Have your resume ready if you are exploring a PJM5900 waiver.
Have your transcript available if you are checking transfer credit options.
